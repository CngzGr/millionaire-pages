Privacy Policy

App Name: Millionaire
Company: appgur (appgur.com)
Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how appgur handles data in the Millionaire mobile app. The app does not offer accounts, sign-in, or user profiles, and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information directly from users.

2) Advertising (Google AdMob Only)

The app uses Google AdMob as the only advertising provider. AdMob may process certain device-related information (e.g., advertising ID, device information, approximate location) for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention according to Google’s policies.

appgur does not build user profiles and does not collect personal data such as name, email address, or phone number. Data processed for advertising is largely controlled by Google/AdMob.

  • Ad provider: Google AdMob
  • Purpose: Ad delivery, ad performance measurement, fraud prevention

3) Data We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect:

  • Identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Account / sign-in / profile information
  • In-app messaging or social profile data

4) Analytics / Tracking

appgur does not use a separate analytics tool to create user profiles or track users based on personal data. Any measurement or tracking related to ads may occur only through AdMob components.

5) Children’s Privacy

The app is not designed to collect personal data from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

6) Third-Party Services

The app includes Google AdMob for advertising. Google’s data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

7) Security

Since appgur does not directly store personal data in the app, there is no dedicated personal data storage process. We still apply reasonable technical measures to keep the app secure.
